About Us

Easy Tiger features artisan bread (including baguettes, croissants, pretzels), locally roasted coffee, housemade meats (sausages, corned beef, pastrami), a full bar and more than 30 local and boutique draft beers.

Stay awhile. It's not just our motto and the message you'll see emblazoned on our restaurant's signage; it's also the philosophy behind every decision we make, and the force that's turned our dream into reality.
